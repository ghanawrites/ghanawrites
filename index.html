About

Ghana Writes is a program designed to promote the works of Ghanaian writers. It is aimed at giving these writers a platform to exhibit their works to the public and also to encourage others to pursue their interests in writing. The objective is to change the myopic mentality that writing is a difficult adventure and to encourage young people to start being creative and write. This program serves as a platform where people can read several forms of articles, poems, stories and get inspired through motivational speeches and videos. Ghana Writes creates an avenue for the world to learn from the creative minds we have in Ghana and appreciate Ghanaian Literature
